ABOUT THIS COMMUNITY
Welcome to the Physics Community!

Here, we converse about our mutual love of Physics! We have weekly online meetings to discuss various topics related to Physics. Each member can bring up a subject of their own, or choose to participate in a conversation someone else has started. We have open discussion boards where members can post questions where they can get help on anything from experimental ideas to homework questions. This community also serves to help our members create valuable professional connections within the online Physics community! If you would like to join, or if you have questions, please feel free to contact us!
